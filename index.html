I am a Research Scientist at Meta, where I work on multimodal large language models and foundation models for personalized content understanding, recommendation, and user engagement.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Boston University, advised by Prof. Margrit Betke. My research interests include multimodal learning, generative models, affective computing, and AI for education. Prior to BU, I received my B.E. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University.
